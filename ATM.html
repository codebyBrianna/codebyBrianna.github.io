All About ATM

About ATM

ATM is a three year computer program at Bancroft Middle School. In this program, students are taught how to use the many programs and wonders of the internet and other devices.

6th Grade Learning

In 6th grade, my first year of ATM, we were introduced to Microsoft Word, how to type well and fast and different ways to be safe on the web. Typing: I was taught how to type, then to progress into typing fast. Microsoft Word: I learned how to make documents and cool tricks I could use when in high school. Web safety: I was introduced into the many different ways to stay safe on the internet.

7th Grade Learning

My 7th gradeyear was the second of my three ATM years. I was taught, how to use SketchUp, FloorPlanner and also how to use photoshop. SketchUp: I learned to design multiple buildings. FloorPlanner: I learned to take measurements, then apply it into designing rooms. Photoshop: I made travel card from Paris, using this, I learned many things here.

8th Grade Learning

In my last of my 3 years of ATM, my 8th grade year, I was learned how to code. I used programs such as Khan Acadamy and Dash to help me with it. I made my own website in Dash, and a blog too.

Reflection

Altogether, the ATM program was very helpful to me. I got to learn about new programs and how to use them. I also got to be able to type faster than I originally did. I know how to code as well which could be useful in the future. Because of ATM I know how to use helpful things I may use in the future. Such as in high school I could use a powerpoint, or a document easily unlike non-ATM students would. The ATM program is very beneficial for me.
